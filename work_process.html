My Web Design Process

What You Can Expect Step by Step

As a BCom Information Systems student, I understand how to bridge technology with real business needs. My process is designed to be collaborative, transparent, and flexible — making it easy for small businesses to establish a strong digital presence without breaking the bank.

02. Strategy Development

After discovery, I’ll put together a tailored plan for your website’s layout, style, and functionality. Here’s what you get:

  • Wire-frames or mockups (if needed)
  • Color scheme and font suggestions
  • Navigation and page structure mapped out

03. Implementation

This is where the real building happens! I bring your vision to life using responsive, modern web development tools. Key highlights:

  • Mobile-friendly and SEO-optimized build
  • Performance-focused design
  • Tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript and CMS platforms if needed

04. Testing & Optimization

Before your site goes live, I test it across browsers, devices, and screen sizes. I also:

  • Check loading speed and fix bugs
  • Ensure accessibility and clear navigation
  • Make any requested revisions

05. Launch & Support

Once you're happy with everything, I publish your site and make sure you feel confident managing it. Post-launch includes:

  • Domain connection and hosting setup
  • Basic training (videos, docs, or a quick Zoom call)
  • Ongoing support for edits, changes, and questions
