Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Common Questions

Can I pay for a website in smaller installments?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans to make it easier for small business owners to get online. You can choose between a once-off payment or an affordable monthly plan over a few months.

I don’t know anything about websites. Can you help me?

Absolutely. We work with you every step of the way—from explaining how websites work to building one that suits your business. No tech knowledge needed!

How long does it take to finish a website?

Most websites take between 1 to 3 weeks depending on your needs and how quickly we receive your content. We'll keep you updated throughout the whole process.

What if I don’t have a logo or pictures?

No problem. We can help create a basic logo and find suitable images for your business at no extra cost. If you want something more advanced, we’ll recommend affordable options.

Can I update the website myself later?

If you want to update your site on your own, we can build it in a way that makes that possible. If not, we also offer affordable ongoing update packages.

Can't find answer to your question?

We're here to help! If you have a question that's not listed above, feel free to reach out. Whether it's about payments, getting started, or anything else — we're happy to chat and make sure you get the support you need.
