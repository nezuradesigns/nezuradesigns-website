About Me

Who I am

Hi! I’m Nurah — a curious, creative, and driven BCom student majoring in Information Systems and Finance at the University of Cape Town.

I’ve always been passionate about technology and how it can make everyday life easier. While searching for businesses in my local area, I noticed a big gap — so many small businesses don’t have websites, which makes it harder for customers to find them online. I did some research and realised that most web design options are far too expensive for many small business owners.

That’s when I decided to step in and offer something different: clean, professional, and affordable websites. I design and build the website first — and only once you’re happy, you pay. It’s all about trust and making things easier for local businesses to get online.

I also started this venture out of necessity — the job market has been tough, and finding an internship in tech hasn't been easy. Instead of waiting for an opportunity, I decided to create my own. This journey has helped me grow both technically and personally, and I’m excited to keep building.

So if you need a website, want to bring your business online, or just want to chat about tech, business, or ideas — I’d love to connect!
